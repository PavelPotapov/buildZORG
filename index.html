UI/UX Technical Designer

Май 29, 2023
Удаленно

We are looking for a talented and skillful UI/UX Technical Designer to work on our game projects for the world’s best publishers and game developers. Pingle Game Studio is a team of professional game developers with over 15 years of industry experience. Our goal is to reach and maintain a leading position among video game developers all over the world. We are engaged in the development and porting, work with all stages of development.

Основные требования:
  • Creating and implementing interfaces from premade design layouts;
  • Compiling and working with interfaces inside game engine (Unreal Engine/Unity);
  • Supporting and correcting the existing interfaces;
  • Creating the structure and work logic for existing interfaces;
  • Adapting interfaces for different platforms: PC, Consoles, Mobile;
  • Offering improvements to the existing interfaces;
  • Optimizing and importing UI assets;
  • Close collaboration with UI/UX designers and game designers;
  • Drawing simple elements of interfaces.
